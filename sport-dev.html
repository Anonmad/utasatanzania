Sports Development Pathways

MADT Elite Pathway Program

The MADT Elite Pathway Program is UTASATANZANIA’s advanced player development track, designed to prepare talented youth (ages 14–21) for competitive football at national and international levels. This boarding program in Mbeya combines training, education, and career opportunities with discipline and values.




What the Program Offers

- High-level training with licensed coaches
- Boarding environment with discipline and routine
- Education support and life skills training
- Exposure to competitions (regional, national, international)
- Sports career pathways including coaching, refereeing, and management
- Talent promotion through scouts, agents, and tournaments like the Norway Cup




The MADT Player DNA

A real MADT player is defined by:
- Value: Respect for the game, coaches, and community
- Passion: Love for football and commitment to growth
- Commitment: Dedication to training and goals
- Hard Work: Persistence on and off the field

Player Requirements

- Age: 14–21 years old
- Valid birth certificate
- Minimum height: 2.5 feet and age-appropriate weight standards
- Personal kit: mattress, blanket, two pairs of boots, and one tracksuit


Points & Ranking System

Players are scored 1–10 points across three pillars:
1. Discipline
2. Talent
3. Hard Work

Top 42 players are ranked on a chart:
- Green (1–16): International tryouts & tournaments
- Yellow (17–30): Strong progress
- Red (31–42): Needs improvement




Development Pathway

1. Foundation Training (14–16 yrs): Technical focus and discipline 2. Performance Phase (16–18 yrs): Tactical awareness and match experience 3. Elite Preparation (18–21 yrs): Advanced training, exposure to scouts 4. Professional Opportunities: Trials abroad, scholarships, career roles in sports



Safeguarding & Wellbeing

The program ensures a safe environment with safeguarding policies, trained staff, medical support, and mentorship for all players.




How to Apply

1. Fill in the application form (online or at UTASA office)
2. Submit player documents (birth certificate, medical form, consent form)
3. Pay registration/boarding fee or secure sponsorship
4. Await trial invitation and selection results
