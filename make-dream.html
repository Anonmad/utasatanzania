Make a Dream Fund (MADF)

About MADF

The Make a Dream Fund (MADF) is an initiative by UTASATANZANIA to support talented youth who face financial or resource constraints. MADF provides scholarships, mentorship, and resources to help young athletes and students participate in sports programs, camps, competitions, and educational activities.

About MADF

The Make a Dream Fund (MADF) is an initiative by UTASATANZANIA to support talented youth who face financial or resource constraints. MADF provides scholarships, mentorship, and resources to help young athletes and students participate in sports programs, camps, competitions, and educational activities.

Vision

Empowering youth to achieve their dreams regardless of financial limitations.

Mission

Remove barriers and provide equal opportunities for talent development and success

How the Application Works:
1. Review eligibility and required documents.
2. Prepare all necessary forms and information.
3. Submit your application via your MADT account if your program allows MADF applications.
4. Track your application status and receive notifications via MADT.

Note

Not all MADT Rising Stars players apply through the same process. Check your program eligibility before applying.

MADF Application Requirements

1. General Eligibility

● Registered in the MADT system.
● Demonstrate exceptional talent or potential in sports, education, or personal development.
● Face financial or resource constraints limiting program participation.

2. Age & Category Requirements

● Meet the age categories specified for the scholarship or program.
● Provide a valid birth certificate or official document for age verification.
● Applicants under 16 years old must have a legal guardian available to provide consent and support.

3. Required Documents

● Completed MADF Application Form (via MADT)
● Code of Conduct Form
● Camp Rules & Regulations Form
● Safeguarding Form
● Health Check-Up Form
● Consent Form from Legal Guardian (for applicants under 16)
● ID Number Submission – All players under UTASATANZANIA hubs must submit their ID numbers via coordinators upon registration.

All documents must be signed by the applicant and, if applicable, by a parent or guardian.

4. Application Process & Period

● Applications must be submitted through MADT if eligible.
● Application Period: 11th December 2025 – 30th January 2026.
● Notifications and updates will be sent via MADT account email.

5. Additional Notes

● Not all applicants may receive funding; selection is based on talent, need, and program availability.
● Applicants must have an active MADT account.
● Commitment to attend programs, training, or competitions is required if selected.
● Compliance with all UTASATANZANIA policies, including safeguarding and health regulations, is mandatory.
● Incomplete applications or missing documents may result in disqualification.

Call to Action Check your eligibility and log in to your MADT account to apply for the Make a Dream Fund if your program allows.
