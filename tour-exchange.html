Tours & Exchange

About the Program

Travel With Us is a UTASATANZANIA initiative that allows children and youth players to experience football beyond their local communities. The program gives players the chance to travel and compete in tournaments across Tanzania, Africa, and beyond. The program is open to: - Children (7–13 years): Eligible for in-country tournaments only. - Youth (13–20 years): Eligible for both in-country and international tournaments. This is a paid program that helps players: • Gain exposure to different levels of competition. • Build cultural awareness and discipline. • Showcase their talent on international stages. • Access scouting and professional development opportunities.

Eligibility Requirements

General Requirements (All Players 7–20) 1. Age Requirement: - 7–13 years → In-country tournaments only. - 13–20 years → In-country and international tournaments. 2. Player Status: Open to both UTASATANZANIA players and external applicants. 3. Documents Required: - Valid birth certificate or passport. - Recent passport-size photos. - Parental/guardian consent (mandatory if under 18). 4. Fitness & Health: - Must undergo a medical check-up. - Provide a health clearance form. 5. Code of Conduct: - Commitment to discipline, teamwork, and respect. 6. Financial Commitment: - Program is fully self-funded by parents/guardians. - Installment payment options may be available.

Program Directory

1. In-Country Tournaments (Ages 7–20) - Participation in national youth tournaments across Tanzania. - Categories: U9, U11, U13, U15, U17, U20. - Examples: Chipukizi Cup, United Talents Cup, SabaSaba Cup. 2. African Tournaments (Ages 13–20) - Regional and continental tournaments across East Africa and Africa. - Exposure to African football culture and competition. - Examples: East African Youth Cup, CECAFA Youth Tournaments. 3. International Tournaments (Ages 13–20) - Opportunities to compete outside Africa in world youth tournaments. - Examples: Norway Cup (Norway), Gothia Cup (Sweden), Dana Cup (Denmark), Dubai Youth Cup (UAE). 4. Cultural Exchange & Learning - Beyond football: travel, new environments, cultural awareness, and leadership development. 5. Player Development & Scouting - Exposure to international scouts and academies. - Opportunities for scholarships and professional pathways. 6. Support & Guidance - UTASATANZANIA staff accompany players for safety and guidance. - Pre-travel orientation sessions on discipline, cultural behavior, and travel etiquette.

Next Steps for Interested Players

1. Fill out the Travel With Us Application Form (available online or at UTASATANZANIA offices). 2. Submit required documents. 3. Undergo assessment and health check. 4. Await selection and confirmation. 5. Complete fee payment process. 6. Begin pre-travel training and orientation.
